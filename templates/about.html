Unmatched Matchup Generator

A quick tool to generate fun and fair Unmatched matchups based on your collection.

← Back to generator

What it does

  • Select the sets you own.
  • Choose playstyles and preferred ranges.
  • Get suggested matchups optimized for your chosen mode (e.g., discovery vs fairness).
  • Optionally lock a fighter and generate tailored opponents.

How suggestions work (high level)

Suggestions are scored using two ideas:

  • Fit: how well a fighter matches the requested playstyles and range preferences.
  • Fairness: how close the matchup is to balanced based on the win-rate matrix.

Different modes can shift the emphasis between fit (discovery) and fairness (balanced matchups).

FAQ

Is this official?

No — this is a fan-made tool for convenience and fun.

Can I share matchups with friends?

Yes — share the URL to this site and the matchup suggestions you generate. (If you later add “share links” that encode selections, it becomes even easier.)
